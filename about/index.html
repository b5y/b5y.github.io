How I got started

My way to computer science is quite typical. I got interested in maths and physics when I studied in secondary school. Being graduated from Presidential Physics and Mathematics Lyceum 239, I decided to proceed my education in one of the most prestigious and famous faculties in Russia - Mathematics and Mechanics Faculty of Saint Petersburg State University (one of the top universities in Russia).

At some point I realized that software engineering due to lack of mathematics at work is quite boring for me and intended to apply for double degree master’s program at ITMO University and University of Amsterdam. After applying for exchange studies at Aalto University I got Research Assistant position in Probabilistic Machine Learning Group under supervision of Distinguished Professor Samuel Kaski. I always wanted to try myself as teacher and was honoured to be a Teaching Assistant for Machine Learning: Basic Principles course tought by Alex Jung. Since that time I decided to stay in Finland.

Passionate about machine learning and ai engineering. Trying to find my path in Deep Learning field.
